About Us

Every day someone somewhere in the world discovers a painting, a drawing, a sculpture or some other work of art of extraordinary value. This is not a rare occurrence. Paintings are being discovered continuously. If you were to read the auction house catalogues of the major auction houses, such as Sotheby’s, Christie’s, Bodnar’s and others, and if you were to read them every month, you would be astonished at how frequently surprising discoveries are made. Nearly every sale includes and reveals a couple of amazing finds. Perhaps you too own a painting, a drawing, a sculpture or some other work of art of exceptional value that has not yet been certified. This is what we do for you. We confirm that authentic works of art are indeed authentic. We study, research, compare and analyze. We perform scientific tests and forensic verifications. And then we deliver evidence – reports and documents – that demonstrate the authenticity of your artwork. Our authentications are internationally accepted as they are based on verifiable evidence as opposed to merely being opinions. Consequently, our clients are able to sell their work of art at the highest price, while at the same time having the choice of selling through an auction house, or privately, or to a museum – anywhere in the world. Should you have any questions about the authentication process, getting a Certificate of Authenticity, selling or what the fees might be, please get in touch with us for the friendliest, most courteous and most informative expert advice. We are contactable via WhatsApp and email. Additionally, we offer a virtual meeting option via Zoom or Skype. As our client, you will have the opportunity to meet with an authentication specialist anywhere that is practical and convenient for you. Or you can simply send us an email first. You will receive a prompt personal response. We are the professional experts that are able and eager to authenticate, certify and appraise the value of your artwork. In addition, if you require assistance with selling, we will provide you with all the help needed.
